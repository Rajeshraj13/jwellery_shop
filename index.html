Frequently Asked Questions.

  • 1. How do I sell old gold?

    Selling your gold with us is a secure, transparent, and efficient process backed by years of expertise. We provide competitive market rates, accurate purity testing, and instant payment after evaluation, ensuring a safe and trustworthy experience for every customer.

    2. How to calculate old gold price?

    To calculate the old gold price, first determine the weight and purity of the gold item. Then multiply the weight by the purity percentage to find the amount of pure gold. Finally, multiply the pure gold weight by the current market gold rate per gram to estimate the total value.

    3. How much wastage is there for old gold?

    Wastage charges usually range from 5% to 7% of the gold weight. For complex or handmade jewellery designs, the wastage may increase to more than 10%. The exact percentage depends on the complexity of the design and the jewellery store’s policy.

    4. What is the process of selling old gold?

    • Step 1: Choose a trusted and licensed gold buyer.
    • Step 2: Carry two valid ID proofs for verification.
    • Step 3: Provide a self-declaration of ownership.
    • Step 4: Allow scientific purity testing of the gold.
    • Step 5: Receive a price based on live gold market rates.
    • Step 6: Review the offer before selling.
    • Step 7: Complete documentation and verification.
    • Step 8: Receive instant payment.
    • Step 9: Collect the transaction receipt.
    • Step 10: Avoid unverified buyers or melting shops.

    5. Is selling old gold taxable?

    Yes. If old gold jewellery is sold after holding it for more than two years, Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) tax of approximately 12.5% may apply without indexation benefits, as per current tax regulations.
